## About

The goal of this project is to provide a comprehensive overview of the current state of research on Temporal Graphs and to curate links to further relevant materials. This resource is intended to help new researchers quickly orient themselves in the field while also serving as a valuable reference for the broader research community to organize and cluster the rapidly growing body of results and publications in this area.

It is an [open source initiative](https://github.com/tgatlas/temporal-graphs-atlas.github.io), and contributions from the community are highly encouraged, such as submitting references or links, suggesting improvements to presentation and organization, or sharing ideas for enhancing the platform. If you have any questions or suggestions, please open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/tgatlas/temporal-graphs-atlas.github.io/issues).

This project was forked from the [Algorithms with Predictions (ALPS)](https://algorithms-with-predictions.github.io/) project by Alexander Lindermayr and Nicole Megow.
